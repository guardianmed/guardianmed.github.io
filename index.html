About Our Idea

Traditionally, to prevent the development of hospital acquired pressure injuries (HAPI), aka bedsores, caregivers must remind patients to reposition every 2 hours. For caregivers, this is time-consuming. For patients - why wait that long if you need assistance with reposition?

The HAPI monitor from GuardianMed can smartly detect patient reposition status, which is then used to generate easy-to-read analytics graphs. Caregivers can check on patients when few repositions have been detected, and save energy to provide other care when patients are turning enough.

Time-saving for caregivers, better care for patients. Simple as that.

AI-powered

Data are collected by ambient devices such as depth camera and IR sensors. It then go through state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms for accurate motion detection.

Visualization-based

Our accompanying mobile application generates easy-to-read visualizations from the collected data; turn times per hour, reposition trend, and need to remind reposition are all clearly displayed in the app.

Privacy secured!

We choose sensors such as depth camera, IR sensors or Lidar, which do not collect facial data We also store all patient data locally, after extensive de-idenfication and other security measures that are compliant with all HIPPA rules.
